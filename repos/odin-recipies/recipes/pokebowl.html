Poke Bowl Recipe

A delicious and healthy Hawaiian-inspired poke bowl with fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors.

Poke bowls are a popular dish originating from Hawaii, featuring marinated raw fish served over a bed of rice and topped with various fresh vegetables and sauces.

Ingredients:

  • 200g sushi-grade tuna or salmon, diced
  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the diced fish with soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice vinegar. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Prepare the sushi rice according to package instructions and let it cool slightly.
  3. In serving bowls, divide the sushi rice as the base.
  4. Top the rice with the marinated fish, avocado slices, cucumber, and julienned carrot.
  5. Optionally, garnish with sesame seeds, sliced green onions, and a drizzle of spicy mayo or sriracha sauce.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh poke bowl!
